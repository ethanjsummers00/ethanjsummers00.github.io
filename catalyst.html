Catalyst Bumper

Objectives

Create television quality bumpers to air alongside the Catalyst Television Program on PBS Arizona, using the existing brand standards, as well as existing iconography. Should be 1080p and last 4 - 8 seconds total.

Process

I first looked over the objectives and existing brand standards/assets for Catalyst. I then developed a storyboard outlining the step by step animation I planned to create. I imported psd and ai assets into Adobe After Effects to create an 8 second bumper with animated rain and icons. I then exported this as a 29.97 fps 1080p video fit for broadcast.
